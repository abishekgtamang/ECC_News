ECC Graduates Enter the Mission Field

Thank you for your faithful prayers, generous support, and unwavering partnership. Because of your encouragement and God's abundant grace, Evangelical Christian College is empowering men and women to lead with Christ-centered teaching and serve faithfully in advancing God's kingdom.

Founded in 2020 through a God-given vision entrusted to Rev. Dr. Kewal Bahadur Khadka (Benjamin), ECC is committed to raising faithful Christian leaders for the Church and the mission field.

We are delighted to share that, Lord willing, on August 20,

2026, ECC will hold its 3rd Graduation Ceremony, commissioning 20 Bachelor of Theology graduates into God's harvest field.

Equipped through theological education, spiritual formation, and practical ministry training, these graduates are ready to serve as pastors, evangelists, church planters, and Christian leaders.

We invite you to continue partnering with us in prayer as we prepare the next generation of Gospel workers for Nepal and beyond.

Dr. Kewal Bahadur Khadka
(Benjamin K.C.)

As they move forward into their designated fields, we humbly ask for your continued prayers on their behalf. Please pray for their spiritual growth, wisdom, protection, and steadfast commitment to God's commission in their lives. We also invite you to partner with them financially, as the ministries of evangelism, church planting, and further ministry training depend greatly on the support of faithful friends and partners.
